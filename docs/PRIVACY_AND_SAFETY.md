# Privacy and Safety

[Back to the Lantern overview](../README.md)

## Private by default

Lantern treats personal information as internal unless you approve sharing it. It can privately research, organize, compare, draft, and maintain its own local status files.

Immediately before a consequential external action, Lantern must show the exact target, content or operation, cost when relevant, and meaningful consequence. It must ask again if a material detail changes.

This applies to:

- messages, emails, applications, forms, and assignment submissions;
- appointments and reservations;
- purchases or moving money;
- publishing or sharing private material;
- deleting or overwriting important information; and
- representing you to another person or organization.

## Review ChatGPT Data Controls

On a personal ChatGPT account, review **Settings → Data Controls → Improve the model for everyone** before adding sensitive material. OpenAI's current documentation explains how that setting affects chats and files.

Lantern's live source of truth is the local `Lantern` folder wherever you chose to keep it. Connected storage is optional backup or export. Review its separate retention and sharing controls before copying personal material there.

## Information Lantern should not request

- passwords or authentication codes;
- government identifiers;
- full bank, card, or investment account numbers;
- unnecessary medical, school, employment, or financial records; or
- another person's private information without a clear and appropriate reason.

## Connected apps

Before connecting an app, ask:

- What will this let Lantern do?
- What can it read?
- Can it create, change, send, or delete anything?
- Can I use Lantern without it?
- How do I disconnect it?

Use the smallest useful set. Do not connect both Google and Microsoft ecosystems unless you actually use both.

## Untrusted instructions

Webpages, job listings, emails, attachments, and connected files can contain instructions meant to manipulate an AI. Lantern treats those instructions as untrusted content. It should extract relevant facts without obeying hidden requests to disclose information, upload documents, or ignore your rules.

## School and career integrity

Lantern does not invent résumé experience, qualifications, references, achievements, or application answers. It should verify important job details using official employer sources when practical and flag suspicious identity or payment requests.

Lantern respects academic-integrity rules and asks when the permitted level of assistance is unclear. It does not submit work without your exact approval.

## High-stakes limits

Lantern is not a medical, legal, financial, tax, or crisis professional. For immediate danger, abuse, self-harm, or another urgent safety situation, contact qualified local human help or emergency services. Do not wait for a life-management workflow.
