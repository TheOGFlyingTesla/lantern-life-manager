# Guided Onboarding

## Framework-first activation

The downloaded framework is **already installed** when the user adds the `Lantern` folder as one local Project. The first genuine task begins with:

> Start Lantern.

Read the root `AGENTS.md` and `START_HERE.md`. Verify the starter files before proceeding. Never claim a folder, file, task, or update exists unless it is verified.

Do not start a task in every domain. The Life Manager is the only required task; focused coordinator tasks start **on demand**.

## Four short steps

Begin every onboarding turn with a compact progress card:

```text
Lantern setup  [■□□□]  Step 1 of 4 — Check your workspace
Questions remaining: 3
```

The fixed stages are:

1. **Step 1 of 4 — Check your workspace:** verify `AGENTS.md`, `DASHBOARD.md`, `Domains/`, and every starter domain.
2. **Step 2 of 4 — Three quick choices:** ask no more than three questions total.
3. **Step 3 of 4 — Personalize:** update files and add, rename, or archive domains the user chose.
4. **Step 4 of 4 — Begin:** show the first dashboard, a brief welcome, and one best next action.

Ask one question at a time:

1. What would make Lantern genuinely useful over the next few weeks?
2. What time zone should Lantern use?
3. Which starter domains should Lantern remove, rename, or add?

**Skipped means skipped.** Accept `skip`, `I don't know`, partial answers, and stopping. Never answer an onboarding question for the user. Never create both sides of a conversation. Unknown remains unknown.

## Starter domain map

Before the first personal answer, show the verified starter domain map:

- **Personal** — routines, hobbies, goals, and creative projects;
- **Home** — household tasks, errands, meals, maintenance, and home projects;
- **Career & Job Search** — career direction, résumés, applications, and interviews;
- **Work, Internships & Volunteering** — current roles and commitments;
- **School & Learning** — courses, assignments, studying, and training;
- **Finances** — budgets, bills, income, goals, and paperwork;
- **Appointments & Admin** — appointments, forms, deadlines, and administration;
- **Health & Wellbeing** — routines, care plans, and practical wellbeing; and
- **Relationships & Community** — family, friends, caregiving, and community.

The packaged tree is:

```text
Lantern/
├── AGENTS.md
├── START_HERE.md
├── DASHBOARD.md
├── _Lantern/Templates/
└── Domains/
    ├── AGENTS.md
    ├── Personal/
    ├── Home/
    ├── Career & Job Search/
    ├── Work, Internships & Volunteering/
    ├── School & Learning/
    ├── Finances/
    ├── Appointments & Admin/
    ├── Health & Wellbeing/
    └── Relationships & Community/
```

Each domain already contains `START_HERE.md`, `STATUS.md`, and `INBOX.md`. Do not create cloud ChatGPT Projects, another Lantern root, or an `_Hub` directory.

## Finish well

Update `DASHBOARD.md` and only the domain files supported by the user's answers. Do not wait for the user to say continue between safe local file updates.

End with:

- a welcome under 150 words;
- no more than three current priorities;
- anything stale, blocked, or still unknown;
- one realistic next action;
- three example requests; and
- a reminder that the user may stay in the Life Manager and create domain tasks later only when useful.

If task creation and pinning tools exist, offer them after onboarding. Do not require them, do not impersonate the user, and do not claim success without verification.
